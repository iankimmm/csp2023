1.1.9 project-- ai taco stand

For our first project, my group created a taco stand. In this game, the plater is greeted and prompted what type of tortilla they want, and what they want as their 2nd and 3rd fillings/toppings. The player must input their choice for the turtle to draw the toppings on the taco. At the end, you can choose whether to make another taco or quit the game.
